Voice Growth Archipelago Privacy

Voice Growth Archipelago is a private habit-tracking app. We store your account profile, your habit definitions, and your completion history so your archipelago can sync across devices.

What we store

  • Your Google account email and display name
  • Your habit names, categories, colors, schedule, and lifecycle status
  • Your daily completion history
  • Your theme preference and onboarding state

What we use the data for

  • Signing you into your private account
  • Syncing your habits and progress across devices
  • Rendering streaks, charts, calendars, and analytics

Deletion

You can request deletion from inside the app and from the public delete-account page. Deletion removes your profile, habits, and completion history from the app database.

Support

For privacy questions or support, contact support@voicegrowth.archipelago.
